Overview

My name is Michael Majdalani and I am currently a Senior in Computer Engineering at the University of Rhode Island. As an Engineering major, I have been taught the skill of lifelong learning, as well as many other engineering skills. I have completed several projects over the past four years, but my interest is mainly in hardware development, firmware, and some web development. My experiences at the University of Rhode Island have prepared me to tackle any problem that appears in the workforce.
